Design Philosophy

Grid Symbol Meaning

  • Melbourne's Layout: Represents the organized suburban structure
  • Community Connection: Each square connects to others
  • Business Network: Professional interconnection
  • You in the Center: White square represents your place in the community

Color Psychology

  • Navy (#0A2540): Trust, professionalism, stability
  • Amber (#B76E00): Warmth, energy, optimism
  • White: Clarity, simplicity, openness
  • Together: Professional yet approachable Melbourne business community
